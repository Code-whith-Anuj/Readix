
Readix : Auto-Scrolling Text Reader 📖


Readix is the ultimate auto-reading experience. Designed for readers who want a hands-free experience, Readix automatically scrolls your text just like watching a short video. Perfect for long articles, captivating stories, and everyday web reading.




✨ Key Features


▶ Auto-Scroll Mastery: Sit back and let the app read to you at your own pace.
⚡ Speed Control: Adjustable scrolling speed from 0.5x to 2.0x for your comfort.
🎨 Custom Themes: Switch effortlessly between light, dark, and sepia themes.
🌐 Multilingual Support: Experience reading in Marathi, Telugu, Hindi, Bengali, and English.
📱 Responsive Design: Fully optimized for mobile and desktop viewing.


🚀 Getting Started


To view the landing page locally:
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser.


📥 Download the App


You can download the latest version of the Readix APK directly from the landing page.
